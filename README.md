# My-First-Project
A self-hosted HTML/CSS/JS productivity tracker to manage daily workouts and study tasks with streaks, progress rings, and a 90-day activity heatmap.
GRIND — Train & Study Tracker
A sleek, single-file personal productivity app built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Designed for anyone who wants to stay consistent with both physical training and academic studies — no accounts, no servers, no fluff.
Features:

Daily checklist for workouts and study tasks with persistent checkmarks
Live progress rings showing today's completion percentage
Streak tracking for training and study separately
Weekly overview and 90-day activity heatmap
All-time stats: total active days, perfect days, best streaks, tasks completed
Auto-resets each new day while preserving your full history
100% offline — everything saves to localStorage in your browser

Tech Stack: Vanilla HTML · CSS · JavaScript · localStorage
Usage: Just download grind-tracker.html and open it in any browser. No installation, no dependencies.
